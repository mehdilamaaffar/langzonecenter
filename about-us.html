About us

LANGZONE center is the fruit of experts in foreign languages teaching, cross-cultural affairs and study abroad. Our traditional style office is located in the colonial part in downtown Rabat, a few minutes walking distance from the 17th century old medina.

Learning

LANGZONE offers convenient learning environment. We keep our classes updated and communicative. This cannot be achieved without our experienced staff in cross-cultural non-formal education.

Interactivity

We believe there is no better way to learn a language than getting involved in interactive activities. In LANGZONE we fulfill the desired learning outcome in this regard through a number of activities - theatre, songs, workshops, presentations, study–trips, and many others.

Discovery

It’s Morocco and the experience here is never complete without excursions. LONGZONE organizes trips to the highlights of magical Morocco. Through our excursions you learn more about the core of Moroccan culture and explore breathtaking landscapes.

“Langzone is host to study abroad program providers including our friends at Academic Programs Abroad. For over 30 years, APA has provided a first-rate study abroad experience that unites academic excellence with the beauty and richness of Francophone culture. We welcome APA students for summer and short-term programs as well as the second leg of the Multi-Country: France, Morocco, Senegal semester program.”
